Our Story
ALPHA
AUTOWERKS

Alpha Autowerks is an automotive performance and service garage in Indore, Madhya Pradesh. Founded with a passion for cars and a commitment to quality, we've grown into one of the most trusted workshops in the region.

Our workshop offers professional servicing, detailing, denting & painting, suspension upgrades, and performance tuning for a wide range of vehicles — from everyday commuters to European performance cars.

Every car that enters our garage is treated with the same level of attention and precision. Whether it's a routine service or a full performance build, we bring the same commitment to every job.
